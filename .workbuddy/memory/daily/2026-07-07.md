## 提示词安全改写

原提示词导致 AI 生成大量英文文字、骷髅、心脏等危险内容。对所有 15 个章节的提示词进行了彻底改写。

改写原则：
1. 每条 prompt 开头必加 "no text, no letters, no words, no numbers"
2. 正向提示词中绝不出现 blood/skull/bone/death/heart/heart organ/horror 等危险词（即使是 "no blood" 也不写，AI 会联想到 blood）
3. 用正面描述替代危险表达：death → adventure continues / camp return / peaceful twilight
4. 通用负向词补全了 alphabet/symbol/signature/any language text/typography/anatomical/corpse/death imagery
5. 强调 "pure visual art" 和 "a reusable UI component, not a complete scene"

已改写章节：2 (通用), 4 (Splash), 5 (MainHub+按钮), 6 (Login), 8 (Character), 9 (AreaSelect), 10 (Shop), 11 (Log), 12 (Settings), 13 (Settlement), 14 (AdventureEnded), 15 (HUD), 16 (DungeonMap), 17 (Upgrade), 18 (Equipment), 19 (Event), 20 (Backgrounds)
